Welcome to MetaLeap! Here's what you get:

MetaLeap is the Open Source-powered, commercial-strength web application server for real-time collaboration and information sharing over world-wide and local networks.

Based upon a rock-solid and steadily improving core platform, choose from an ever-growing range of pluggable solution packages, (by design always fully customizable) to easily compose and seamlessly combine advanced document management, content management or other types of information management applications.

Integrate your MetaLeap applications and solutions painlessly with emerging web technologies such as social software, ever-smarter REST web service APIs and powerful widgets / mash-ups for document viewing, geo-location / maps and so much more.

About this ReadMe.txt

This ReadMe.txt is designed to guide you through the first steps of installing, setting and configuring MetaLeap instances. It is not the Full Product Documentation. Once you get MetaLeap up and running, that documentation will be available easily from its web interfaces.

Installation on Linux and Mac OS X:

  • Extract the MetaLeap setup package (MetaLeap.zip) to any location in your file system.
    • This almost completes your installation! MetaLeap runs on these folders and files you just extracted. This forms the root directory of your MetaLeap instance.
